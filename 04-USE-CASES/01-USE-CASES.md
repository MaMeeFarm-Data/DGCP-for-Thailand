# DGCP™ Use Cases for Thailand  
**Real-World Applications Across Agriculture, Labor, Welfare, and AI**

---

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines practical use cases of DGCP™  
within the Thai national context.  
DGCP™ transforms Real-World Data (RWD) into a verifiable,  
human-owned asset that supports economic development,  
ethical AI, and transparent governance.

These use cases demonstrate how DGCP™ can  
improve public services, strengthen rural economies,  
and provide accountability across sectors.

---

## 2. Agriculture & Rural Development

Agriculture is the core of Thailand’s economy,  
yet farmers lack traceable, verifiable records of their work.

DGCP™ enables:

### 2.1 Proof-of-Farming  
Farmers generate timestamped logs of:

- planting  
- feeding  
- harvesting  
- weather conditions  
- livestock care  
- resource usage  

This proof supports:

- crop insurance  
- subsidy verification  
- land rights disputes  
- farm-to-market transparency  

### 2.2 Product Traceability  
Agricultural products gain:

- provenance  
- quality assurance  
- export readiness  
- anti-counterfeit protection  

### 2.3 Rural Data Cooperatives  
Communities can pool RWD and negotiate better prices  
for agricultural products using verified proof.

## 3. Labor & Informal Economy

Nearly half of Thai workers are informal and undocumented.

DGCP™ provides:

### 3.1 Proof-of-Work-Life  
Workers generate verifiable logs of their daily activities:

- construction  
- delivery  
- caregiving  
- service work  
- domestic labor  

Supports:

- micro-loans  
- fair wage negotiations  
- social protection programs  
- legal evidence  

### 3.2 Portable Labor Identity  
A worker can carry their RWD record job-to-job  
without depending on an employer.
